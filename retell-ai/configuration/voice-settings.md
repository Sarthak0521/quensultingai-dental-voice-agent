# Voice Settings

# Overview

This document describes the voice configuration used by the AI Dental Receptionist in Retell AI.

The voice settings are configured to provide a clear, professional, and friendly experience for patients calling the dental clinic.

---

# Voice Characteristics

The selected voice is configured to be:

- Professional
- Friendly
- Calm
- Clear
- Easy to understand

The objective is to simulate the experience of speaking with a real dental clinic receptionist.

---

# Speaking Style

The AI should:

- Speak naturally.
- Avoid rushing.
- Pause appropriately after asking questions.
- Pronounce appointment dates and times clearly.
- Maintain a conversational tone.

---

# Design Considerations

The voice was selected based on:

- Clarity
- Natural pronunciation
- Professional tone
- Patient comfort
- Reduced listening fatigue
